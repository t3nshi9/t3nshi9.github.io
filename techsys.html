PC Parts

There are 7 main pc parts. The CPU (Central Processing Unit) is the brain of the computer. The motherboard connects all the components together. The RAM (Random Access Memory) alows data to be stored and accessed from any location within the memory. The PSU (Power Suply Unit) is the heart of the computer. The GPU (Graphics Proecessing Unit) is responsible for rendering and displaying videos, images and animations. An SSD/HDD (storage device) provides long term local storage.Finally you have a computer case to hold the components and also manage airflow and cooling.

Peripherals (Input)

  • Keyboard - Allows you to input letters, numbers and commands.
  • Mouse - Allows you interact with the computer by controlling the pointer and clicking on stuff.
  • Webcam - Takes videos of you for video calls, streaming or recording. Can sometimes capture audio as well.
  • Microphone - Captures audio which is then typically used for communication or recording.

Peripherals (Output)

  • Monitor - Displays the computers output with things like text, images and videos.
  • Printer - Produces a physical copy of documents or images. (Also known as a hard copy)
  • Speakers - Plays audio from your computers out loud.
  • Headphones - Plays audio from your computer but for personal listening either over the ear or in ear.
